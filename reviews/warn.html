Warn Industries Review

The undisputed king of winches since 1948

★★★★½ 4.8/5.0

Products: Winches, bumpers, mounting systems, recovery accessories

Our Full Review

When people say "winch," they usually mean Warn — that's how dominant this brand is. Founded in 1948, Warn has been building recovery equipment longer than most off-road companies have existed. The ZEON and VR EVO series are industry standards, and their newer electric winches offer impressive power-to-weight ratios. They've expanded into bumpers and mounting systems, but winches remain their core competency and their reputation is bulletproof.
