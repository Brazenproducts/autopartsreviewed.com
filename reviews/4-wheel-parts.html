Our Full Review

4 Wheel Parts bridges the gap between online shopping and hands-on service. With over 100 locations across the U.S., they offer something most online retailers can't: professional installation. Buy a lift kit online, schedule an install at your local store, and show up with coffee. That convenience has real value. Their online prices tend to run a bit higher than pure e-commerce competitors, but the install option and ability to see/touch products in person makes up for it if you have a location nearby.

What We Like

  • 100+ physical locations with install bays
  • Buy online, install in-store option
  • Excellent for tires and wheels
  • Knowledgeable staff at most locations

What Could Be Better

  • Prices higher than online-only competitors
  • In-store experience varies by location
  • Website could be better organized
  • Install labor costs add up

The Bottom Line

4 Wheel Parts earns its #6 spot in our rankings with a solid combination of reliability and value. Worth checking for specific needs, especially if their strengths match what you are looking for.
