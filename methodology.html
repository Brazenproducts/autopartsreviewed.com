How We Rank Auto Parts Stores

Transparency matters. Here's exactly how we evaluate and rank the stores on this site.

Our Criteria

  • Product Selection (25%) — Catalog depth and breadth across categories
  • Pricing (20%) — How prices compare to MAP, MSRP, and competitors
  • Shipping Speed & Cost (15%) — Order processing time, delivery speed, free shipping thresholds
  • Website Experience (15%) — Ease of use, search quality, fitment tools, mobile experience
  • Customer Service (15%) — Response time, knowledge, return process
  • Reputation & Trust (10%) — Years in business, community reputation, BBB rating

Our Process

We place real orders from every store we review. We time how long it takes from order to delivery. We test the return process. We call customer service with questions. We compare prices across stores on identical products. We do this at least twice per year for our ranked stores.
