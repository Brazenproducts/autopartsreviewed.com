About AutoPartsReviewed

We're car enthusiasts who got frustrated with fake "best auto parts stores" lists that are just SEO bait. So we built the resource we wished existed — honest, detailed store reviews backed by real purchases.

Every store on our site has been tested with actual orders. We track shipping, evaluate customer service, process returns, and compare prices. Our reviews are updated at least annually.

Revenue: We earn commissions through affiliate links. This doesn't influence our rankings — stores cannot pay for placement.
